Tiles#

Background Tiles#

Space#

Empty space that can be passed through.

Lava#

Kills the player on contact.

Circuit#

The only tile elevators can move on.

Generic Tiles#

Block#

Just a regular block that can be walked on and pushed. Nothing too special.

Spawn#

Where the player starts. There can only be one.

Escape#

Where the player ends.

Elevator#

Moves along circuits, Stops when powered.

Hotrock#

Direct source of power.

Sticky Elevator#

Modification of the elevator that sticks onto and drags tiles it touches.

Diamond#

Type of escape. When collected, the spawn of the level becomes an escape.

Glass#

Type of block. Transparent.

Magma#

Type of lava. Spreads over time.

Mechanical Tiles#

Switch#

Sends out a signal of its assigned ID when pressed by the player or a falling tile.

Door#

Toggles every time it receives a signal. While active, blocks off all motion and power for tiles it covers.

Turret#

When powered on any side, fires bullets out of the opposite side at an assigned frequency.

Light#

When powered on any side, shines lighting out of the opposite side.

Piston#

When powered, pushes out its piston rod and whatever is in front of it.

Piston Rod#

When powered, leaves its piston and pushes whatever is in front of it.

Dropper#

When powered or signalled, releases a single droplet. Can only have one active droplet.

Conveyor#

When powered, pushes all low-weight tiles on it in an assigned direction.

Conductor#

Emits power when powered externally, otherwise inert

Grappler#

When powered, fires out its hook and pulls itself to whatever it hits, emitting power along the way

Hook#

Fired by grapplers and leaves behind a trail.

Sensor Tiles#

Sensor#

Base class of all sensors.

Transformer#

Converts signals into power; when signalled, it switches between being a power source and a regular tile.

Broadcaster#

Converts power into signals; When it gets or loses power, it sends out a signal.

Picker#

Converts droplets into signals; it consumes touching droplets and sends out a signal whenever it does.

Tripwire#

Converts motion into signals; whenever the player runs through it, it send out a signal.

Projectiles#

Bullet#

Fired from turrets. Kills the player on hit and dies on collision with a solid tile.

Lighting#

Casts light. Summoned from lights.

Droplet#

Small falling tile produced by a dropper. Consumed by pickers.

Hook Trail#

Left behind by hooks.
